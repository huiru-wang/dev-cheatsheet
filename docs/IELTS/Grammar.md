# 语法复习
## 基本语法成分
能够构成句子的最基本的成分有以下几种：

1、主语

2、谓语动词/**非谓语动词**(不定式、过去式、现在分词、过去分词)

> I love you.
> 
> He is going <font class="font-pink">to swimming</font>.

3、宾语

4、宾语补足语(补语)

I am going to paint it <font class="font-pink">pink.</font>

5、主语补足语(补语，修饰主语时又叫表语)

> He is <font class="font-pink">smart</font>.

6、定语

> The little white rabbit ate <font class="font-red">a large</font> carrot.

7、状语(通常为副词词性)

> The rabbit ate <font class="font-orange">quickly</font>.

8、同位语

> Jack, <font class="font-green">an English teacher</font>, likes swimming.

## 复合句和复杂句
### 复合句

将基本的句子进行简单叠加，是并列关系；通常由**连词连接**；

选择并列句：

`I would have written to you, <font class="font-pink">but</font> I have been sick.`

转折并列句：

`Neither does he smoke nor does he drink.`

联合并列句：

`Give him a inch and he will take a mile.`

因果并列句：

`She promised me, so she makes it./ She makes it, because she promised me.`

### 复杂句

句子进行嵌套，是从属关系；通常**将简单句修改成从句形式，嵌套进主句**中构成；

- 名词性从句(使用从句的形式代替名词)
  - 主语 👉 主语从句：<font class="font-orange">What we need</font> is time.
  - 宾语 👉 宾语从句：I believe <font class="font-orange">she is honest</font>. 
  - 表语 👉 表语从句：China is no longer what <font class="font-orange">she used to be</font>.
  - 同位语 👉 同位语从句: I had no idea <font class="font-orange">that you were here</font>.
- 状语 👉 状语从句：The news is exciting <font class="font-orange">so that she jumped up</font>.
- 定语 👉 定语从句：He is the man <font class="font-orange">who I saw yestoday</font>.

  如：have、can、might、be、do、must

## 谓语动词的时态和语气
时态、语气都是用在**谓语动词**上，表达不同时间、状态的动作、动作的假设、动作的情感、可能性等；
### 时态
- 时间：过去、过去的未来、现在、未来
- 状态：一般、进行中、已完成、完成进行中

时态进行排列组合，理论上会有16种：

| 状态\时间 | 过去      | 过去的未来      | 现在      | 未来                                   |
| --------- | --------- | --------------- | --------- | -------------------------------------- |
| 一般      | did       | would do        | do        | will do                                |
| 已完成    | had done  | would have done | have done | will have done<br>will have been doing |
| 进行中    | was doing | would be doing  | doing     | will be doing                          |
| 完成进行  |           |                 |           |                                        |

## 句型补充
- 陈述句
  > I am Iron Man.
- 疑问句、反问句（回答仅遵从事实而非提问方式）
  > Do you .. ?
  > 
  > Don't you .. ?
- 祈使句
  > Be quiet!
  > 
  > Have a good day.
- 倒装句
  > So do I./ So does he. / Nor do I. / Either did I.
  > 
  > There is a cat.
- 强调句
  > It **was** he who won the game.
  > 
  > I **do** agree with you.
- 感叹句
  > What a nice day!
  > 
  > How cute the cat is!
- 虚拟语气
  > I wish I **were** there.
  > 
  > I **should have already learned** English well.
- 插入语：表补充强调，删除不改变原意
  > The reason, of course, is that costs have rocketed.
  > 
  > I prefer to go tomorrow afternoon, at 6 pm.
- 双重否定
  > You can not fail to obey it.
  > 
  > I can not agree more.
  > 
  > **There is no denying** that everyone was happy.
